Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bike?

Booking a bike is easy! Simply go to our Booking page, select your preferred bike model, choose your rental dates and times, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What are your operating hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm. Bike pickups and returns can be arranged 24/7 using our self-service kiosks at all locations.

Do you offer discounts for long-term rentals?

Yes! We offer special weekly and monthly rates that provide significant savings compared to daily rentals. Contact our sales team for custom quotes on rentals longer than one month.

What safety measures do you have in place?

All our bikes are thoroughly sanitized between rentals, and we provide complimentary helmets and sanitizing wipes with every rental. Our bikes also feature GPS tracking and theft prevention systems.
